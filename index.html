About Us

Our Vision

To create a vibrant and inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem at SIT Hyderabad that nurtures innovation, enables start-ups, and empowers students, faculty, and alumni to become visionary entrepreneurs and change makers.

Our Mission

  • Inspire and support the entrepreneurial aspirations of SIT stakeholders.
  • Institutionalize innovation as a core value across all activities.
  • Enable pre-incubation, incubation, and funding support for impactful startups.
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration and national/international linkages.

Our Strategic Initiatives

We are building a robust ecosystem to support every stage of the entrepreneurial journey.

Governance & Structure

A dedicated E-Cell guided by a multi-stakeholder committee of faculty, alumni, and industry leaders.

Pre-Incubation & Infra

Access to a dedicated E-Cell office, pre-incubation lab, and innovation spaces on campus.

Innovation Support & Funding

An Innovation Fund to support prototyping, patent filing, and seed grants.

Flexible Startup Policy

Academic credits, semester breaks, and relaxed attendance for students pursuing their ventures.

Curriculum & Pedagogy

Hands-on workshops, design thinking training, and guest lectures embedded within the curriculum.

Ecosystem Engagement

Strong linkages with T-Hub, NASSCOM, TiE, and an active alumni network.

I&E Policy

Our commitment to empowering innovators is codified in our institutional policy.

We foster a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem, constitute a multi-stakeholder committee, create a sustainable financial model, and engage with national and international innovation networks to drive institutional innovation.

We provide access to E-Cell facilities, facilitate collaborations with regional incubators, support IP filings, and allow academic flexibility (semester breaks, credits) for students to work on their startups. Faculty can also take sabbaticals to pursue entrepreneurial ventures.

Students representing SIT at recognized entrepreneurship events or actively involved in validated ventures may be considered for attendance exemption, subject to prior written approval from the Director, based on formal application and endorsement.
